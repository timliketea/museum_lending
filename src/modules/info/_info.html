FAQs Stone Essence museum

  • Can visitors take pictures in the museum?

    Visitors are welcome to take pictures in the museum to capture and cherish their memorable moments. Please be mindful of any photography guidelines in place. Enjoy your visit and happy snapping!

  • What types of artifacts can be found in the museum?

    In our sculpture museum, you can discover a rich collection of diverse artifacts, showcasing an array of sculptural masterpieces. From classical marble statues to contemporary abstract sculptures, the exhibits offer a fascinating journey through the world of three-dimensional art. Explore the beauty and craftsmanship of sculptures representing various styles, periods, and cultures.

  • What time is the museum open?

    The museum is open to visitors during the following hours:

    Monday to Friday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM Closed on Sundays

    Please note that these hours are subject to change, so it's recommended to check our official website for the most up-to-date information. We look forward to your visit!

  • How can I get involved or support the museum?

    Support our museum by becoming a member for exclusive benefits. Volunteer your time, donate artifacts, or contribute financially to enrich our cultural heritage. Attend events, spread the word, and join us in preserving history.
